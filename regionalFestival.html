Holi, a symbol of unity and joy, is celebrated with enthusiasm by our community every year. We organize a colorful gathering where people from all walks of life come together to play with colors, share sweets, and enjoy cultural performances. The event promotes social harmony and reminds us of the triumph of good over evil. Our volunteers ensure eco-friendly colors and safe practices are followed throughout the event.

Our NGO organizes inclusive celebrations where underprivileged families can participate and experience the joy of community. We distribute sweets, clothes, colors, lamps, food, and gifts during every occasion.

Through these efforts, we spread happiness, preserve culture, and build a sense of unity across all backgrounds.

Diwali at Purvanchal Bhawan is a grand celebration filled with lights, devotion, and charity. We illuminate our center with earthen lamps, perform Lakshmi Puja, and distribute sweets and clothes to underprivileged families. Through community donations, we ensure that even the poorest homes experience the brightness and warmth of Diwali. Special awareness drives are also held to encourage a cracker-free and environmentally conscious celebration.
