Health Checkup Camp

At Purvanchal Jan Kalyan Sangh, we are committed to improving community health by organizing regular Free Health Check-Up Camps across rural and underserved areas. These camps provide essential medical services including free consultations with qualified doctors, basic tests such as blood pressure, sugar, and BMI checks, along with the distribution of necessary medicines at no cost. Special attention is given to women’s and children’s health, ensuring that even the most vulnerable receive the care they need. Through these initiatives, we aim to raise health awareness, encourage early detection of illnesses, and make quality healthcare accessible to those who need it most. With the support of medical professionals and generous donors, our recent camps have benefited hundreds of individuals. We invite volunteers, sponsors, and well-wishers to join us in making a meaningful difference in the lives of many.

Blood Pressure Monitoring

Checks for high or low blood pressure to help detect early signs of hypertension or other heart-related issues.

Dental Check-Up

Oral examination to identify cavities, gum issues, and promote good dental hygiene practices.

Eye Check-Up

Vision testing and screening for common eye issues like blurry vision, redness, or irritation.

Blood Sugar Test

Quick screening to check blood glucose levels and identify early signs of diabetes.

Cardiac Screening

Basic heart health check including pulse, heart rate, and ECG (if available) to detect early signs of heart issues.

Health Awareness Sessions

Educating people on hygiene, nutrition, and prevention of lifestyle diseases like diabetes and hypertension.
