About Us Purvanchal

Purvanchal Bhawan is a dedicated social organization committed to the upliftment and welfare of the people of the Purvanchal region and beyond. Founded with a vision to build a strong, united, and self-reliant community, we work tirelessly to support underprivileged individuals, promote education, preserve culture, and provide essential aid during times of need. We believe in the power of togetherness. Our team, made up of compassionate volunteers and social workers, organizes regular health camps, educational support programs, cultural events, food distribution drives, and relief initiatives during natural calamities. Our goal is simple — to bring smiles, spread awareness, and empower lives through genuine care and community-driven efforts.

" At Purvanchal Bhawan, every step we take is toward a brighter, more inclusive future.
Join us in building a better society — because together, we rise. "

Our Mission & Vision

★★★★★

Our Vision

  • To build a compassionate and inclusive society.
  • To ensure dignity, equality, and opportunity for every individual.
  • To strengthen community bonds through social unity and mutual support.
  • To preserve the cultural roots and values of the Purvanchal region.
  • To become a trusted platform for meaningful and lasting social change.

Our Mission

  • Organize free health camps, awareness drives, and blood donation events.
  • Promote cultural programs to celebrate Purvanchal’s heritage.
  • Empower women, children, and the elderly through dedicated welfare activities.
  • Encourage volunteerism and community participation at the grassroots level.
  • Offer emergency relief during natural disasters and crises.
