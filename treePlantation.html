üå± Tree Plantation

As part of our commitment to environmental sustainability, Purvanchal Bhawan Jan Kalyan Sangh regularly organizes Tree Plantation Drives to promote greenery and combat climate change. These drives are conducted in schools, public parks, roadside areas, and rural communities, engaging volunteers of all ages. Our mission is to raise awareness about the importance of trees in maintaining ecological balance, purifying the air, and providing shelter and food to countless species. Each planted sapling represents a step toward a cleaner, greener future. With the active participation of students, local residents, and officials, we aim to instill a sense of responsibility towards nature and inspire a culture of environmental conservation in society.

üå≥ Adopt-a-Tree Campaign

Our Adopt-a-Tree Campaign encourages individuals and families to take personal responsibility for a sapling. After planting, they commit to watering and nurturing the tree until it matures. This fosters a personal connection with nature and helps ensure a higher survival rate for planted trees. Participants receive a certificate of appreciation and regular guidance on tree care. This initiative not only boosts tree survival rates but also spreads the message that every small effort counts in preserving our environment.
