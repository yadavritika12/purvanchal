Marriage Support for the Underprivileged

Purvanchal Bhawan Jan Kalyan Sangh firmly believes that every individual deserves dignity, love, and support—especially during life’s most important milestones. One of our key social initiatives is providing marriage support to underprivileged families who struggle with the financial burden of wedding ceremonies. Through this program, we extend financial assistance, essential wedding items, and logistical help to families who are unable to afford the basic necessities for their children’s marriage. Our team identifies deserving cases through community outreach, and with the help of generous donors and well-wishers, ensures a respectful and joyous celebration.

  • • Financial support for marriage expenses
  • • Providing clothes, gifts, and essentials
  • • Organizing community wedding events
  • • Partnering with local vendors for arrangements
  • • Empowering women through a fresh start
