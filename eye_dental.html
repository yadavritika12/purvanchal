Purvanchal Bhawan Jan Kalyan Sangh organizes free Eye Check-Up Camps to provide essential eye care services to the underserved. These camps are conducted by a team of professional ophthalmologists who carry out comprehensive vision screenings, eye pressure checks, and preliminary tests to detect cataracts and other common vision-related issues. Many individuals in rural or low-income communities often ignore signs of deteriorating eyesight due to lack of awareness or resources. This camp addresses those gaps by offering not only free consultations but also distributing spectacles to those in need. Educational materials and sessions are also provided to promote eye care awareness and encourage early diagnosis. The goal of these camps is not just to treat vision problems but also to empower individuals with the knowledge to take better care of their eyes.

üò∑ Dental camp

Purvanchal Bhawan Jan Kalyan Sangh also conducts free Dental Check-Up Camps to improve oral hygiene awareness and access to dental care in underserved areas. With the help of experienced dentists, these camps provide dental screening, consultation, and minor treatments such as scaling and cavity detection. Many people suffer silently from dental issues like toothaches, gum infections, and decay due to lack of dental care facilities or financial constraints. The camp aims to fill this gap by offering preventive and curative services free of cost. Participants are educated about daily oral hygiene practices, effects of poor oral care, and are encouraged to adopt healthy habits. These camps make a lasting impact by reducing preventable dental diseases and encouraging routine dental check-ups among communities that often go without any form of oral healthcare.
